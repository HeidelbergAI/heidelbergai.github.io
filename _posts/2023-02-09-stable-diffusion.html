Latent Diffusion Models such as Stable Diffusion and OpenAIs DALL·E are revolutionizing the way we process images, achieving unprecedented visual fidelity without the need for excessive computational power. This technology is already being used in countless apps and has captured the attention of the AI community and beyond. We are excited to have Robin Rombach, the first author of this groundbreaking work which originated here in Heidelberg, speaking at the DKFZ. Don't miss out on this opportunity to learn about cutting-edge Computer Vision.

To prime yourself for potential use cases of Stable Diffusion, visit the Stable Diffusion subreddit.
